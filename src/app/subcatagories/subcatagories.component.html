What is Creping?

Creping is the fundamental part of the tissue process: taking the flat sheet and adding the 3-D crepe structive to give the bulk, absorbency and handfeel properties tissue customers require by a combination of chemical and mechanical actors.

Benefits

Any tissue application can reap the benefits of high-performance Duroblade. Whether you are producing toilet tissue, facial, napkins or coffee filters, with the right doctor blade you can:

  • increase your process efficiency and lower your costs
