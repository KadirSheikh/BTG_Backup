PROdry Yankee dryer services

PROdry thermal imaging technology has been continuously developed over the last 16 years, with over 100 machines audited from most major tissue making groups, with many machines audited multiple times.

PROdry's infrared analysis technology records a stop-action image of the Yankee over one revolution, synchronised with a known reference point to provide a precise location map of the Yankee dryers thermal properties. This allows the engineer to identify various components of the condensate removal system and diagnose any issues which may exist. These may need correction during a planned maintenance shut, or settings may be adjusted during the audit. Along with a review of other critical dryer key performance indicators, tests may be made with dryer variables, such as blowthrough velocity and differential pressure in order to optimize the thermal profile.

BTG PROdry precision linescan thermal imaging is used by tissuemakers globally to troubleshoot Yankee issues under normal operating conditions, without the need for shutdown. Amongst the questions we can answer with a PROdry audit are:

  • Is chatter damage occurring due to a Yankee internal or steam system operating condition?
  • Is a particular header of straw(s) damaged?
  • Is my CD profile issue Yankee related?
  • Is my MD profile issue Yankee related?
  • Is my blowthrough or delta P control working correctly?
  • Is my thermo compressor operating correctly?
  • What is the cause of my blistering issue?

CBC

What is CBC?

CBC is creping blade control.

With CBC, the blade automatically follows the profile of the Yankee cylinder surface and ensures an even linear load along the blade length. The blade holder pivots around the blade contact point on the Yankee surface to adjust working angles.

What BTG Does? BTG makes it easy!

BTG CBC Blade holder has a host of unique features no other blade holder on the market can offer:

  • Self-adjusting blade
  • Separate blade load adjustment at the edges
  • Easy and continuous adjustment of blade height
  • Independent adjustment of linear load and creping pocket angle while running

BTG also offers a comprehensive service contract encompassing expert maintenance and upgrades, to help keep the CBC in mint condition and optimize its performance.
